Must Watch TV Shows

-By Aditya Narayan Shahi

Written on 29th August,2017



Watching TV shows is the favourite pastime of every college student. So here I present to you a list of 10 must watch TV shows.

  1. Game of Thrones (2011-)

    2. Game of thrones is one of the most awesome TV shows I have ever seen. It's about people of different houses fighting against each other to claim their rule over Seven Kingdoms.



  2. Friends (1994-2004)

    3. Friends is an epic comedy TV series which is a story about six friends and their life incidents which is a whole package of humour.



  3. Breaking Bad (2008-2013)

    4. Breaking Bad is TV series based on crime and drama. In this, a chemistry teacher fighting with inoperable cancer gets into meth buisness with one of his former students leading to change in his priorities.



  4. House of Cards (2013-)

    5. House of Cards is a political drama. Every character in this series can double-cross anyone. The protagonist, Frank Underwood is shown as a master puppeteer in politics taking revenge against all those who betrayed him.



  5. Suits (2011-)

    6. Suits is an awesome drama series wherein one of New York's best lawyer Harvey Specter hires a young college dropout Mike Ross who didn't had a law degree. They both are shown handling cases together while hiding Mike's secret.



  6. Westworld (2016-)

    7. Westworld is a fictious plot based on human creativity and tendency to create a higher self being. It shows how things can become worse when your own creation turn against you.



  7. The Originals (2013-)

    8. It is an American drama TV series. It is based on a vampire family which settles in the city of New Orleans and the challenges they face in their quest for more power.



  8. How I Met Your Mother (2005-2014)

    9. It is a popular sitcom in which Ted (the protagonist) narrates to his children the story of how he met their mother, going past through all his life incidents with his friends.



  9. Prison Break (2005-)

    10. It is a suspense-thriller. It shows how an engineer gets himself into a prison to help his brother escape.



  10. Impractical Jokers (2011-)

    11. It is a hidden camera reality-show where four lifelong friends give each other embarassing tasks to perform. This show will definitely tickle your funny bone.



Note: Everything written above are my personal views.
